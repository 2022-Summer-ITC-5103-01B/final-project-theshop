What is it?

Halo Infinite is the foundation for a new generation of Master Chief fights and a stunning return to form.

Why should you play it?Halo Infinite may have drawn influence from open world video games like Borderlands, Fallout, and Far Cry, but 343 Industries' vision of a larger area is completely unique. When Master Chief is given free reign on Zeta Halo, the action never stops and never fails to make you smile. Halo Infinite never fails to find the joy, whether you're firing at groups of deftly-written Banished adversaries, mantling and grappling across a gorgeously inhospitable alien environment, or travelling restlessly from one mission to the next in an effort to stop an extinction catastrophe that's endangering mankind. An outstanding, competitive multiplayer experience complements the superb story, with 343 taking the famed online play of Halo 3 and updating it for a new generation of players. Since we saw the Fall of Reach, Halo has improved in terms of how it looks, sounds, and plays.
