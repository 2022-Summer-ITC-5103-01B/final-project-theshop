Hitman3

$75.99

What is it?

A creative stealth game in which anything and everything should be utilised as a weapon.

Why should you play it?
Any game that allows you to step into the role of an accomplished assassin, that necessitates the wearing of several disguises, and that considers toilet pans and fibre wire equally as murderous tools is unquestionably deserving of consideration for Game of the Year. Naturally, the stealth shooter from IO Interactive has all of the aforementioned features and is also really enjoyable. After resuming Agent 47's career in 2016, IO gradually expanded the breadth and creativity of the bald butcher's adventures throughout the following years. Hitman 3 is the culmination of that journey. From the soaring heights of Dubai's Burj Al-Ghazali to the dimly lit depths of Berlin's Berghain (or "Club Holle," as it is known here), Hitman 3's expertly crafted settings keep you guessing from beginning to end and each one offers up a myriad of killer tools to exploit in your attempt to paint the town walls red.

Developer: IO Interactive
Format: PC, PS4, PS5, Xbox One, Xbox Series X
